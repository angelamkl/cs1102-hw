Discovery

What was new for us?

The biggest discovery was that recommender systems are not one model, but a pipeline of simpler steps. Candidate generation, scoring, and re-ranking each solve a different problem.

We also learned that LLMs are not only for chatbots. They can summarize item text, explain recommendations, and support ranking when a system needs more language understanding.

Reflection

What this project taught us

Technical lesson

Simple HTML, CSS, and JavaScript are enough to build a clear teaching tool if the content is structured carefully.

Design lesson

A guided story and short sections are easier to understand than one long block of text.

Learning lesson

Flashcards are a practical way to reinforce the key ideas without adding a backend.

Ethics lesson

Recommendation quality alone is not enough; privacy and fairness need to be considered from the beginning.
