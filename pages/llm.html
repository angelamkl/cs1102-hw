Roles

What can an LLM do?

Summarize text

An LLM can turn reviews, descriptions, or chat text into concise signals that help a recommender understand the item better.

Generate explanations

It can produce human-friendly reasons such as “Recommended because you like mystery films and this one has a similar setting.”

Support ranking

In some systems it helps score or re-rank items by reasoning over user and item descriptions.

Benefits and limits

Why businesses are interested

Benefits

  • Better use of unstructured text such as reviews.
  • More natural explanations for users.
  • Useful when item metadata is incomplete.

Limits

  • Can be slower than simpler models.
  • May show unstable outputs when prompts change.
  • Can be affected by position bias and noisy text.
  • LLMs can add text understanding to recommender workflows.
  • They are useful for explanations and feature extraction.
  • They also add latency and reliability concerns.
